TRIVA

Did You Know ?

TuteDude was founded in 2021 by a group of passionate educators and food enthusiasts who wanted to create a unique learning experience that combined education with delicious food.

Fun Fact

TuteDude's signature dish, the "Crunchy Veggie Taco," was inspired by a popular street food in Mexico and has become a fan favorite among customers.

Trivia

TuteDude's menu is designed to cater to a wide range of dietary preferences, including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options, ensuring that everyone can enjoy a delicious meal while learning.
